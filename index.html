Wireman MCQ App

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026
01

Introduction

Wireman MCQ App is an educational application developed to help ITI Wireman students, trainees, apprentices, and technical learners prepare for examinations through practice questions, mock tests, and progress tracking.

The application is designed to operate fully offline and does not require an internet connection for normal use.

By using Wireman MCQ App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

02

Information Stored on Your Device

To provide core application functionality, Wireman MCQ App stores certain information locally on your device, including:

  • Language preference
  • Theme preference
  • Selected study year
  • Onboarding status
  • Bookmarked questions
  • Quiz progress
  • Chapter progress
  • Test history
  • CBT practice history
  • Premium access status
  • Other application settings necessary for normal operation

This information is stored only on your device and is used solely to provide application features and functionality.

03

Personal Information

Wireman MCQ App does not require user registration.

The application does not collect, request, store, or transmit personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Residential address
  • Photographs
  • Contact lists
  • Messages
04

Data Collection

Wireman MCQ App does not collect user data on external servers.

The application does not operate user accounts, cloud storage systems, or remote databases for storing user study information.

Progress, bookmarks, preferences, and other application data remain stored locally on the user's device.

05

Internet Usage

Wireman MCQ App is designed to function fully offline.

An internet connection is not required for:

  • Studying questions
  • Taking tests
  • Viewing progress
  • Accessing bookmarks
  • Using CBT practice features
  • Accessing educational content included within the application

All question content, study material, progress tracking, bookmarks, and test functionality included in the application are available offline after installation.

06

In-App Purchases and Premium Features

Wireman MCQ App offers an optional Premium subscription that unlocks additional features within the application.

Payment transactions are processed entirely through Google Play Billing.

Wireman MCQ App does not handle, receive, store, or have access to any payment card information, UPI information, banking information, or billing credentials.

Payment-related information is managed by Google according to Google's policies and terms.

07

Third-Party Services

The application may interact with the following platform services where applicable:

  • Google Play Store
  • Google Play Billing

There are no third-party advertising, analytics, or user-tracking services included in the application.

08

Data Removal and User Control

Users maintain full control over locally stored application data.

Available options may include:

  • Reset Progress
  • Clear application data through device settings
  • Uninstall the application

Uninstalling the application removes locally stored application data from the device, including progress, bookmarks, and preferences.

Eligible Premium purchases may be restored using the Restore Purchase feature provided through Google Play Billing, subject to Google Play policies and account eligibility.

09

Educational Use

Wireman MCQ App is intended for educational and examination preparation purposes for ITI students, trainees, apprentices, and other technical learners.

10

Data Security

Most application data is stored locally on the user's device.

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices and protecting access to the application.

11

Any future updates will be published with a revised “Last Updated” date.

Continued use of the application after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
